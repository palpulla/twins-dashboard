# Operations Manager / Integrator at Twins Garage Doors

*Candidate-facing one-pager. Use this in the screening call follow-up, as the link in recruiting emails, or share with anyone evaluating the role.*

---

## What this is

A founder-direct **Integrator** seat at a profitable garage door business in Madison, WI, run by twin engineer-brothers based in Cambridge, MA. We are building toward a sale to private equity in 2 to 3 years. You would be replacing our fractional COO and elevating the role.

We run on EOS. You would be the Integrator to our Visionary.

---

## Where to focus

Four priorities, in order:

1. **PE exit readiness.** Codify the business so a buyer can read it.
2. **Streamline operations.** Measurable systems, not founder vigilance.
3. **Team growth and accountability.** Hire, onboard, hold the line.
4. **Relationships in our markets.** Local presence, lead flow.

---

## The day-to-day, in one paragraph

In the trenches with our technicians, CSRs, and dispatchers. Driving the team against KPIs and SOPs across dispatch, CRM, margin, and field ops. Hiring and onboarding more technicians and dispatchers as we open new locations. Using AI aggressively wherever it removes manual work. Forming local relationships that drive lead flow. Working directly with both founders. Running weekly L10s. Owning the outcome.

---

## What success looks like

**By 90 days:** Team is hitting KPIs (or visibly trending up on the laggards). Each person owns their numbers. Communication and accountability rhythms are tight. Local partner relationships are forming, with 1-2 already producing leads. You have met everyone and ridden along.

**By 12 months:** Hiring engine repeatable. New location stable. Margin up, manual time down. Partner-driven lead flow is real. PE-ready.

---

## Who fits

You see problems and fix them without permission. You are comfortable with no-SOP-yet. You finish what you start. You can build trust with a 50-year-old technician and an MIT-trained founder in the same hour. You will pick up the phone at 7 AM. You have actually used AI in real work. You want a 2-to-3-year arc that ends in something meaningful.

---

## Logistics

- **Remote.** Founders in Cambridge, business in Madison. Occasional travel to Madison.
- **Full-time preferred.** Internship-with-FT-path open if you are a current grad student.
- **Compensation flexible.** Equity on the table for the right full-time hire.
- **Reports to founders directly.**

We have not hired this role before. You will shape it with us. The pace is fast, the timeline to exit is real, and the right person owns the outcome.
